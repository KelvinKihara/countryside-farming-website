❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer fresh milk delivery to homes in Nyeri? +
Yes! We offer daily fresh milk delivery to homes, hotels, and businesses within Nyeri County. Contact us on 0724688438 to set up a delivery schedule and discuss pricing for your needs.
What breeds of dairy cows do you keep? +
We primarily keep Friesian (Holstein) and Ayrshire breeds, known for their high milk yield and adaptability to the Kenyan highlands climate. Our cows are sourced from reputable breeders and regularly tested for health and productivity.
Can I buy day-old chicks from your farm? +
Yes, we periodically have day-old chicks (DOCs) available for both broiler and layer strains. We also offer starter feeds and vaccination packages. Please call ahead to check availability and reserve your order in advance.
Do you provide farming training or consultation services? +
Absolutely. We offer one-on-one farming consultations, farm visits, and structured training programs for aspiring and existing farmers. Topics include dairy management, poultry husbandry, feed formulation, and business planning for farms.
Is your farm certified or registered? +
Yes, Countryside Farming Ventures Ltd is a fully registered business in Kenya. We comply with all Kenya Dairy Board (KDB) regulations for milk production and follow Kenya Veterinary Board guidelines for animal health management.
How can I place a bulk order for products? +
For bulk orders of milk, chickens, eggs, or farm produce, please contact us directly via phone at 0724688438 or send an email. We offer special pricing and flexible delivery arrangements for bulk purchasers, schools, hotels, and supermarkets.
